Employees

Employee Status
Each member of our team plays a crucial role in the success of our shop. Whether you are on the floor helping customers, managing stock, or handling behind-the-scenes operations, your contributions are essential. We want to ensure everyone have any concerns or ideas for improving your role, has the tools and support they need to succeed. If you ever please feel free to reach out.

Work Environment
Our goal is to foster a positive and respectful work environment where all employees feel valued and supported. We are committed to maintaining a safe, inclusive, and motivating space for everyone to thrive. Your well-being matters to us, and we continually seek ways to improve your daily work experience.

Salary & Benefits
We understand that fair compensation is key to ensuring our team feels appreciated. Your salary is determined by your role, experience, and performance. We strive to be competitive within the industry, and as we grow, we are committed to offering opportunities for salary reviews and raises based on your performance. Additionally, we provide benefits that are aimed at improving your quality of life, and we encourage you to take full advantage of them.

Work-Life Balance
We are aware of the importance of maintaining a healthy work-life balance. We encourage all employees to take their time off when needed and to communicate with management if you ever feel overwhelmed. Your mental and physical well-being is important, and we want to ensure you are able to recharge and return to work feeling energized.

Career Development
We believe in investing in our team. We offer training, mentorship, and opportunities for growth within the company. If you are looking to take on more responsibilities or move up in your career, we are here to support you and help you achieve your goals.
